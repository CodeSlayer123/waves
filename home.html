ABOUT WAVES INC.

Why do we sell Waves?

Responsibility

For too long the ocean has gotten away with recklessness and endangerment of lives with no oversight.
Our goal is to change this, one beach at a time.

Joy

In our innocence as children, we all loved the beach, blind to the horrors of the great depths, the loss wrought by the uncaring tides. We want to bring that joy back to adults.

Wildlife

Fish and sea life are oft neglected until they end up on a plate. For every Wave purchased, part of the money will go to repairing ocean habitats to provide healthier fish to eat.

Money

We love money, just like everyone else.
Selling Waves helps nature and our pockets.

We know Waves.

Our team members each have decades of experience with the ocean.
Navigating the unforgiving currents, subduing the righteous Waves in the Antarctic, riding the California Mavericks
We've done it all.
